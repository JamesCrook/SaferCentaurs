Who This Community Is For

Our Discord is designed for educators, content creators, and developers who are building tools using AI. Think 3Blue1Brown, Wikipedia diagram contributors, educational content creators, and software developers exploring human-AI collaboration patterns.

We're particularly interested in connecting with people who:

  • Create educational content and need better diagram tools
  • Build tools that combine human creativity with AI capabilities
  • Contribute visual content to Wikipedia or other knowledge platforms
  • Develop software with AI assistance while maintaining human control
